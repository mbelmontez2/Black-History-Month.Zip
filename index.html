Mike Pondsmith,born on April 14th, 1954 as Michael Alyn Pondsmith in Santa Cruz, California would go on to find prominence in the tabletops games industry after founding R.Talsorian Games in 1982.

The first game Pondsmith designed from the ground up was Mekton, a mecha game with heavy manga and anime influences, released in 1984. The game's first public testing occurred at a local convention. After seeing Blade Runner his company would begin development on CyberPunk 2013 which would release in 1988
